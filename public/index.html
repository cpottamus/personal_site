About Me

Who I am

I'm a 21 year old on his way out of Northwestern, and I want to build. I'm deeply interested in finding ways to make a positive impact on society, particularly through fields that use new technologies to empower people. I think the evolution of education and our accessibility to knowledge will define the future. Passion and creativity are what I value most in my work.

Great products are built when creativity meets an accurate conception of need. I believe both of these traits are consequent to the sum of your experiences, and so I work hard to maximize my exposure to the world.

What I do

I love taking an idea from inception to implementation, and I've gotten to play many roles in that process. I have filled product management-style positions at 3 companies, and contributed as a software developer as well. Beyond employed experience, I work with friends of similar ambitions to build our own entrepreneurial projects.

Aside from that, I'm a fervent musician, outdoorsman, sailor, and runner.
